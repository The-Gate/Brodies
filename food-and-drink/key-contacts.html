Key contact

Eric Galbraith

Partner, Head of Food & Drink, Brodies LLP

Phone:

+44 (0)141 245 6253

Email:

eric.galbraith@brodies.com

Eric brings a wealth of specialist international expertise to the corporate/M&A team. He is widely regarded as a leading lawyer in his specialist field, having built a formidable reputation for delivering on international projects on behalf of clients.

A corporate lawyer of nearly 30 years' standing, Eric specialises in mergers & acquisitions, joint ventures and international franchising, regularly advising clients in the food and drink industry and in the public sector on corporate structures, with an emphasis on charities and social enterprises.

Chambers UK independent legal directory have commended Eric as a lawyer who "genuinely has his clients' interests at heart", "is very accurate and understands situations rapidly," and "always comes up with the right solution."
