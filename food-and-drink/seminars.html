Session 1: Seminar Option 2

Holiday pay – is a ticking time bomb about to explode?

Paul McMahon and Alison Locke, Brodies LLP

The issue of historic and on-going underpayment of holiday pay is vexing employers up and down the country. Billed as the ‘next PPI’, mass litigation is looming with unions and employees lodging grievances and gearing up to lodge Employment Tribunal claims. It has the potential to have a material impact on many organisations’ bottom lines in the food and drink sector. This session de-mystifies the law. It explains where we are, what is likely to happen, and most importantly, what practical steps can be taken by employers to brace themselves for the impact.
