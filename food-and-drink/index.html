The Brodies Food & Drink Conference returns to Perth Racecourse in November 2018

In what has been another exciting year for the Scottish food and drink sector, join us for our annual food & drink conference on Friday 9 November.

Our guest speakers and panellists will share their views and experiences on a wide range of topics relevant to this vibrant sector. A full programme, along with speaker details, will be announced in due course. In the meantime to register your free place at the conference, please email events@brodies.com or call Dawn Stanley on 0141 428 3345.

We look forward to welcoming you to Perth.
