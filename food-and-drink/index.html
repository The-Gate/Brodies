The Brodies Food & Drink Conference returns to Perth in November 2017.

Join us at our annual food and drink conference at Perth Racecourse.

This year's conference theme will be growth. We are witnessing a period of unprecedented prosperity in the Scottish food and drink sector, with exports valued at £1.2 billion for the first three months of 2017 - a year-on-year increase of £124 million. 2016 was a record year for food and drink and this trend shows every sign of continuing.

Our conference will provide an opportunity to hear from industry experts on how they have grown their businesses and plan further expansion, whether by achieving their own strategic objectives or through greater industry collaboration.

Further speakers and the full conference programme will be announced in due course, but in the meantime we are delighted to confirm the following industry experts:

  • James Withers, Chief Executive – Scotland Food & Drink
  • Ton Christiaanse, UK Chief Executive – Brakes Group
  • Dougal Sharp, Founder & Chief Brewer – Innis & Gunn
  • Tony Lamb, Head of Food & Drink – Department for International Trade

To register your free place at the conference please email events@brodies.com or call T: 0131 656 0054 if you have any queries.

We look forward to welcoming you to Perth.
